Research

SPES 3U CubeSat

March 26, 2019

Cells are constantly interacting with their surrounding extracellular matrix (ECM). They spread, contract, and migrate in these polymer networks. We are interested in developing tools to better define the mechanical properties of the ECM and to understand how cell-matrix interactions affect cell behavior.

Pressure Regulator for Organ-On-a-Chip

June 10, 2019

Optical methods are essential for visualizing physiological and pathological processes. Our group has been developing optics-based instrumentation to enable new ways of probing the mechanical properties of cells during disease and development.
